About Me

KC

I'm a passionate electrical engineer and researcher pursuing my MSE at the University of Pennsylvania through the Vagelos Integrated Program in Energy Research. Currently working on cutting-edge research in device physics and autonomous systems.

My work spans from characterizing ferroelectric diodes for next-generation memory applications to developing autonomous vehicle systems using ROS2. I'm particularly interested in the intersection of hardware and software, bringing together my knowledge in semiconductor physics, machine learning, and embedded systems.

When I'm not in the lab, you'll find me mentoring students as a TA, working on aerospace projects, or exploring new technologies. I'm always excited about projects that push the boundaries of what's possible.
