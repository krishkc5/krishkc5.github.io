About Me

Hi, I'm Krishna. I build tools and systems across hardware and silicon design. My background spans digital and analog circuits, memory architectures, and device physics, from SystemVerilog verification to Cadence simulation to real hardware characterization.

I'm particularly drawn to problems where abstractions break down: memory behavior under variability, verification bottlenecks, and the tradeoffs between accuracy, energy, and latency at the hardware-software boundary. Outside of chip design, I also work on autonomous systems and embedded platforms.

When I'm not debugging simulations or in the lab, you'll usually find me playing tennis or watching far too much YouTube when I should be sleeping :D
