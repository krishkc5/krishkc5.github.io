About Me

I'm a student researcher specializing in circuit design for semiconductor memory technologies and device physics. My research focuses on ferroelectric diodes for next-generation non-volatile memory, combining fabrication, electrical characterization, and computational modeling to push the boundaries of memory technologies.

Beyond the lab, I work on autonomous systems, machine learning, and hardware-software integration. Between Cadence simulations and lab meetings, I enjoy playing tennis and watching YouTube videos when I should really be sleeping :D
